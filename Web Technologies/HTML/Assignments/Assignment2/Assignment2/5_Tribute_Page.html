Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Missile Man of India

Achievements

  • 11th President of India (2002–2007)
  • Played a key role in India's nuclear tests in 1998
  • Worked with ISRO and DRDO to develop missiles like Agni and Prithvi
  • Author of best-selling books like "Wings of Fire"
  • Recipient of Bharat Ratna, Padma Bhushan, and Padma Vibhushan

Dr. Kalam was known for his humility, vision for India's youth, and inspiring leadership. He always emphasized the importance of dreaming big and working hard to achieve those dreams.
